 What is a Video Game? Video games are games that have the user interact with an electronic device that displays pictures to show the gameplay. One large impact videogames have had recently is how they have stepped out from just being experienced by being the one playing the game. Watching streams of people playing video games and videos of people playing them are popular forms of entertainment, along with the numerous movies and shows that are set in the worlds of popular games.
