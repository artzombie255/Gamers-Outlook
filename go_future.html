 Video games seem to be moving in a direction of increased immersion with VR and AR systems. As well as better graphics in general. In such a short time games were taken from the pixelated arcade games of the 80s and into the high graphics games of now like GTA 5 and other similar games. There is also a trend where games are taking up more and more space on computers. The increased graphics have a hand in this as better graphics require more storage space. Additionally, there is a trend where games are getting more and more expensive. This is especially clear with Nintendo Switch games going up in price. When the Switch was first released Nintendo brand games could be around $60 but lately, it has been $70 or greater.
